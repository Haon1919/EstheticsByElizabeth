Get to know me:

I'm a cat lover, outdoor enthusiast (when it's not freezing!), and a fan of shopping and spending time with my fiancée, family, and friends! A couple of years ago, my struggle with cystic acne sparked a deep passion for skincare. After trying what felt like everything, I finally discovered a routine that worked for me—and that's when my journey really took off.

In July 2024, I enrolled at Summit Salon Academy to become a licensed esthetician, graduating that December. Now, I'm so excited to launch my career at Confidence Renewed LLC, where I can grow and help others feel confident in their skin!
